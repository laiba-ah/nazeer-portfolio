Nazeer Ahmed Qureshi

CEO/Founder

Nazir Ahmed Qureshi is a disciplined and purpose driven individual who values integrity, continuous learning, and personal growth. Beyond his professional role as CEO, he maintains a balanced lifestyle centered on family, faith, and self improvement. He believes in creating a positive environment both at home and in society, reflecting his strong moral principles and commitment to excellence. Nazir is passionate about mentoring young professionals, contributing to community development, and staying engaged with emerging trends in technology and education. His personal life reflects resilience, humility, and a forward-thinking mindset, which strongly influence his leadership style and vision.

Background & History

With over two decades of progressive leadership experience, he currently serves as Chief Executive Officer at All In 1 Solutions (2020–Present), where he leads the organization with a strategic focus on strengthening internal audit frameworks, enhancing corporate governance, and delivering high-impact professional training programs to drive operational excellence. He has been served as Executive Director at NCBMS since 2014, overseeing large-scale business operations and successfully executing strategic initiatives for over 11 years. Earlier in his career, he worked as Manager HR & Finance at Deloitte (2007–2014), where he managed complex financial verifications, account reconciliations, and corporate audit engagements for international clients. He began his professional journey in 2003 as a Finance & Admin Officer at NCBMS, where he played a key role in coordinating academic and administrative functions, with a strong emphasis on process efficiency and student service excellence.
